# Title

A Conversation with Dr. Christian Geiser on his journey as a quantitative psychologist

# Abstract

This moderated Q&A session features Dr. Christian Geiser, former Professor of Quantitative Psychology and current Director of Education for Quantfish (https://www.goquantfish.com/), an independent statistics training platform for researchers and graduate students. After a successful academic career, Dr. Geiser transitioned into statistics consulting and instruction, where he now develops new courses and manages the Quantfish business alongside his wife, Ginger. Dr. Geiser will share insights from his journey through undergraduate and doctoral training to academia and independent instruction. This session is designed as a Q&A for graduate students to learn about the diverse career paths available to Quantitative Psychology students through firsthand experience. Participants are encouraged to ask questions about careers in consulting, instruction, and industry.
